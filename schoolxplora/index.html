About SchoolXplora


SchoolXplora is the first ever online event organised exclusively for school students from grade/standard 6-12. Self Development Club of Kongu Engineering College mainly focuses to bring out the talents of school children through the online portal. This event has both technical and non technical subcategories which explores the cultural, art and academic skills of the school students. In addition, the best performer of every event will be awarded with the virtual momento which will motivate the students to further develop their skills.
